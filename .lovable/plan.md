

# Eng Indhagod — Premium Portfolio Website

## Overview
A multi-page, enterprise-level portfolio website for **Xasan Cabdi Yusuf (Eng Indhagod)** — a tech startup-style personal brand showcasing full-stack development, design, multimedia, and marketing services.

**Brand Slogan:** *Building Digital Experiences That Inspire, Convert & Grow Businesses.*

---

## Design System
- **Colors:** White (#FFFFFF), Royal Blue (#2563EB), Teal Accent (#14B8A6), with dark mode variants
- **Fonts:** Poppins/Montserrat for headings, Inter for body text
- **Style:** Clean, minimal, glassmorphism cards, subtle gradients, professional spacing
- **Dark/Light Mode:** Toggle with smooth transition, preference saved to localStorage
